Purpose:
Provide a juke box/music thing.

Required Functionality:
1. The loading of music.
2. The playing of music.
3. Playing all music in a directory.
4. Playing all music in subdirectories of a directory. (Needs to be configurable)
5. Manage all playing music.
6. Pausing music.
7. Skipping music.
8. Volume adjustment.

Namespace:
None is needed due to encapsulation provided by OO programming. So, it's part of the sound module's namespace.
